\paragraph{Requirements:}\

	Patient appointment \& Scheduling system will help in organizing appointments and selecting the best
	doctors for patient to see. This system should reduce time and make running hospital more efficient

	\begin{itemize}
	\item Patient information is collected in order to determine most suitable doctor/department to assign
	the patient.

	\item Fixing the schedule based on the availability of the supplier

	\item Automated reminders to both the patient and physician.

	\end{itemize}
